About Us

Protea Teens is a passionate youth-led mental health advocacy group based in Florida. We are dedicated to combating the rising teenage suicide rates by advocating for increased mental health resources within high schools.

Our team consists of motivated individuals who are committed to making a positive impact in our community. We believe in the power of peer support, education, and activism to create meaningful change.

Together, we strive to raise awareness, reduce stigma, and provide support to those affected by mental health challenges. Join us in our mission to promote mental wellness and empower young people to lead healthier, happier lives.
